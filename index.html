Research Areas

My research focuses on the convergence of quantum computing and classical computing systems, developing novel algorithms and hardware solutions for the quantum era.

Quantum Error Correction

Developing hardware accelerators for quantum error correction algorithms, including FPGA implementations of the Sparse Blossom Algorithm for real-time quantum error correction.

Electronic Design Automation

Creating quantum algorithms for Boolean function matching in EDA toolchains, achieving super-polynomial speedups for circuit design and library mapping problems.

Hardware Acceleration

Designing reconfigurable architectures and GPU-based frameworks for biomedical applications, quantum computing, and high-performance computing systems.

Quantum Algorithms

Developing novel quantum algorithms for combinatorial optimization, including QAOA implementations and quantum annealing approaches for NP-complete problems.
